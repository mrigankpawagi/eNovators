Group Discussion

Number of participants per team: 1
Classes: IX to XII

Time Allowed: 60 Minutes

• Event will be conducted in two rounds: preliminary and final.
• In the prelims participants have to speak for 1minute on the given topic.
• Result of the prelims will be declared on the spot and only shortlisted students will participate for the final round.
• The decision of the judges will be final and will not be subjected to any change.
• The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic instruments.

Enovators

eNovators is the IT Club of Vishwa Bhararti Public School, Noida - founded in 2003 - where students brainstorm in different IT activities like programming, web designing, quizzing, graphic designing, etc. The club is open to students from classes 9 to 12, in the school.
